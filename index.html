Calibration

What this instrument measures — Founder Dependency Analysis™ evaluates the extent to which your organisation’s decision-making, identity, operations, and external relationships are structurally dependent on the founder. It examines eight dimensions: founder identity centrality, decision authority concentration, relationship dependency, operational reliance, leadership structure maturity, strategic direction ownership, cultural authority, and succession readiness.

Why the signal matters — High founder dependency increases risk during transition, scaling, or absence. Understanding where dependency is concentrated helps leadership prioritise institutionalisation and build resilience without losing the founder’s strategic value.

How responses will be analysed — Your ratings (1–5 on each statement) are combined using a weighted scoring engine. Dimension scores and an overall dependency index are computed; reverse-scored items ensure that institutionalised practices lower the dependency signal. Results are reviewed by Kaylee-Jane and synthesised into a diagnostic report delivered within 24 hours.
